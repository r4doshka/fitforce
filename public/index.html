FitForce.com App for Trainers

Whether you’re an experienced personal trainer looking to simplify your business or interested in starting a new own fitness training business, FitForce.com has much to offer.

With FitForce.com App for Trainers, you can schedule and manage your training sessions, manage invoices, and track the success of your clients with your phone or computer. Everything is sent in real-time and stored in the cloud. There’s no need for large filing cabinets filled with paperwork anymore. Help save the environment and your sanity by going digital.

FitForce.com App for Trainers is on schedule for release in early 2018.

Our Features

Communications

With FitForce.com you can easily keep track of all client or trainer contact information and directly communicate with your them through our messaging feature.

Invoicing

You never have to miss a payment again. Easily manage and send invoices directly through the FitForce.com app and stay in the know about which client has paid, and which client needs a reminder.

Scheduling

It’s easy to stay on top of all personal training sessions with our built-in scheduling tool. FitForce.com will send both you and your client a notification before your training session begins.

Designing Programs

Design and update personal health and fitness programs unique to your clients’ individual needs directly through the FitForce.com app. We’ll send everything to your clients in real-time.

Health and Fitness Tracking

You can easily stay on top of your clients’ progress through our health and fitness tracking feature. Their results will be sent directly to you in real-time.

FitForce.com App for Clients

Working with clients should be simple, with FitForce.com App for Clients it's a breeze. Take the customer experience to the next level by inviting your clients to download the app on their phones FREE of charge. FitForce.com App for Clients enables you to provide an exceptional all-around customer experience to your clients while helping them achieve their health and fitness goals.

Clients who choose to use FitForce.com Clients app on their mobile devices will be able to record their health and fitness measurements, log their workouts, view and track their progress, and share their workout logs and fitness records with you at will. As their trainer, you will be able to communicate, sent automated notifications for appointments and invoices, share personalized fitness programs and routines, and seamlessly stay on top of their progress.
